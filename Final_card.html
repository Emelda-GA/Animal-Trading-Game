Bulldog

Thirty-nine American universities use a Bulldog as their mascot, the most famous being Uga Lee (known as Uga), the University of Georgia's mascot.

  • Scientific Name: Canis lupus familiaris
  • Average Height: 1–2 ft (30–61 cm)
  • Average Lifespan: 8-12 years
  • Color: Brindle; solid white, red, fawn or yellow; piebald

The Bulldog, also known as the British Bulldog or English Bulldog, stands 1-2 feet tall and is recognizable by its loose skin, furrowed brow, and pushed-in nose. It is the 5th most popular breed in 2019 according to the American Kennel Club. Males average 50-55 lb. (23-25kg) and Females average 40-50 lb. (18-23kg).
